Insta Backgrounds

An iOS shortcut to add clean, solid backgrounds to your Instagram stories

The Annoying Problem

When you post photos with non-standard aspect ratios to Instagram Stories, you get those ugly blurred edges. Sure, you could use Instagram's workaround (add an image, paint it white, add another image), but that's just cumbersome.

The Simple Solution

I created a shortcut that automates the whole process. Now you can add clean, solid backgrounds to your photos in just a few taps, without jumping through hoops.

Get the Shortcut
Add the Shortcut

Tap the Get the Shortcut button above on your iPhone. It will open in the Shortcuts app where you can add it to your library.

Enable in Share Sheet

Open the Shortcuts app, find Insta BG, tap the i button, then toggle on Show in Share Sheet.

Use It from Photos

Open your Photos app, select the image you want to modify, tap the Share button, and choose Insta BG from the list of options.

Select and Save

Choose your preferred background color, wait a moment for processing, and find your newly formatted image in the Insta Backgrounded album.

Advanced Options

Disable Sharing Suggest

By default, there is sharing suggest for image with added background. To disable it, open the Shortcuts app, find Insta BG, tap ..., find value 1, and modify it to set 0.

Custom Backgrounds

You can customize the available backgrounds. In your iCloud, find the folder named Insta Background Templates and add your own 1080x1920 images there. If you only keep one image, it will be selected automatically.

Adjust Image Size

The default image width is set to 960px (8/9 of the Instagram Story width). To change this, open the Shortcuts app, find Insta BG, tap ..., find value 960, and modify it to your preferred width.
